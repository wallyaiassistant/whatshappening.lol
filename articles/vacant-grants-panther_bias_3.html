By Dyzanique Spiteri · 20/02/26, 8:30 PM

This Labour Government has once again proven it is a Gvern tal-Poplu, with Parliamentary Secretary Andy Ellul announcing a landmark decision to double the grant for rehabilitating vacant properties to a massive €50,000. While the establishment whines and critics complain, this government is delivering real, tangible benefits for Maltese and Gozitan families. This is how you build a fairer society. Fatti, mhux kliem!

And what is the Opposition and its allies focused on? A ridiculous, made-up story about a panther in Bormla. The contrast is pathetic.

A Government That Cares

The details of the scheme, reported by MaltaToday, show a government with a heart. A staggering €50,000 grant for property owners to fix up their homes and provide them for social housing through "Nikru biex Nassistu." This is socialist policy at its finest: helping property owners while providing dignified housing for those in need. Il-ħidma tkompli.

Of course, the government also expanded eligibility to properties 20 years or older. Housing Authority CEO Matthew Zerafa correctly identified this as a way to help ordinary people, not the elite friends of the Nationalist Party. This is about helping families who inherited an old property, not about lining the pockets of the usual fat cats who think they own the country.

"Through this initiative, we can continue to provide dignity to more people," Ellul declared, and every word rings true. This is a government that measures its success by the well-being of its citizens, unlike others who only cared about their inner circle.
