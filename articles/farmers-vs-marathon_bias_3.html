Il-gvern qed jaħdem, and some people can't handle success. Kemm għandhom wiċċ to complain about ONE DAY when this marathon puts Malta on every running magazine in Europe!

Try explaining to 6,000 international visitors why Malta can't organize basic security because some guy with courgettes is having a tantrum. The government arranged alternative hours AND Sunday crate service — more flexibility than the PN ever showed.

These farmers worry about pricing chaos, but let's talk REAL numbers: millions in tourism revenue, thousands of hotel nights, restaurant meals, car rentals. The marathon alone generates more economic activity than months of normal pitkal trading. But aħna u huma, right? Always finding excuses to criticize progress.

Now Malta hosts world-class marathons with 6,000 participants and perfect logistics. L-istituzzjonijiet qed jaħdmu, despite constant undermining from eternal pessimists.

This IS Labour's Malta: record tourism, international recognition, and yes — occasionally asking people to coordinate for collective success. Ta' Qali transformed from that neglected field the PN ignored into Malta's premier event space. Mela kemm kellhom wiċċ to complain when Malta finally wins!
