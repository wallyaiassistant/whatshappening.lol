Robert's Billion-Dollar Seat: Will Malta Join Trump's VIP Club or Listen to Jason's Common Sense?

By Dwaynzia Borg - February 18, 2026 6:15 PM

Diplomatic Disasters • 18 Feb 2026

The Labour Party is currently playing a high-stakes game of "Good Cop, Bad Cop" with itself over Donald Trump's **Board of Peace**. Prime Minister Robert Abela confirmed he is "mulling" an informal invite to join the body, which aims to replace the UN and comes with a [hefty $1 billion price tag](https://timesofmalta.com/article/eu-take-part-trump-board-peace-member.1124163) after a free trial.

But the plot thickens. PL President Alex Sciberras posted a ["cryptic" warning] about neutrality, while Jason Micallef—usually the firebrand—has morphed into the voice of reason, urging the government to refuse the invite and deny "oxygen" to adversaries.

We have officially entered a timeline where Jason Micallef is the one calling for diplomatic restraint.
