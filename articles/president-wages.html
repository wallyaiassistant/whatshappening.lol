President Solves Voter Apathy: "Have We Tried Paying Politicians More?"

By Shanazaya Vella - February 18, 2026 12:00 PM

Democratic Deficit • 18 Feb 2026

In a visionary leap of logic that has left political scientists baffled, President Myriam Spiteri Debono has identified the root cause of Malta's declining voter turnout: MPs aren't getting paid enough.

Speaking on TVM's XTRA, the President suggested that proper remuneration is the key to attracting talent and, presumably, exciting the electorate. Because nothing motivates a disillusioned voter quite like knowing the person they are voting for is securing a bigger bag.

While the President also touched on "might is right" politics and international law, the takeaway for the average citizen was clear: The solution to a democratic deficit is a financial surplus... for the politicians.
