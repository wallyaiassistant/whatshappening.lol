The PA has approved the expansion of the Fawwara quarry — granting a 10-year permit to operators that the Court of Appeal ordered evicted for illegally occupying public land. Courts don't matter anymore. Not under Labour.

This is the same government that killed Daphne Caruana Galizia's legacy, buried the Panama Papers, and turned Malta into a corruption playground for connected oligarchs. Now they're literally overriding court decisions to reward their developer friends. Aħna u huma — except "huma" are the courts, the law, and the people, and "aħna" is the Labour machine and its cronies.

Charles Polidano — Iċ-Ċaqnu — co-owns this quarry. The same Polidano whose name surfaces in every major development controversy on this island. The same circles that bankrolled Labour's rise, that benefited from every institutional blind eye since 2013. Ġebel Ciantar's farmland was sacred once — given to farmers in 1933. Now it's being devoured by a quarry because Labour's PA serves developers, not citizens.

The PA case officer didn't just overlook the court order — they erased it entirely from the assessment. This isn't incompetence. This is systematic corruption. The same pattern we've seen since Muscat's Panama gang took over: capture the institutions, appoint loyalists, and the machine does the rest. Abela changed the face but kept the engine.
